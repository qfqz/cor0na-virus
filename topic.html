Corona Virus Research

What is Coronavirus?

Coronavirus is a family of viruses that cause illnesses ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases like COVID-19. It was first identified in Wuhan, China, in December 2019.

Symptoms

Common symptoms include fever, cough, and shortness of breath. In severe cases, it can lead to pneumonia, kidney failure, and even death.

Prevention

To prevent the spread of coronavirus, wash your hands frequently, wear a mask, maintain social distancing, and avoid large gatherings.

Vaccines

Vaccines like Pfizer, Moderna, and AstraZeneca have been developed to combat COVID-19. They help build immunity and reduce the severity of the disease.

Impact on Society

The pandemic has caused significant disruptions to daily life, including school closures, remote work, and economic challenges.
